The Cuttlefish

The cuttlefish is an amazing creature. It could be considered a master of disguise with its ability to camouflage itself. Not only does it change colors to match its surroundings, but it also changes its texture and both of these can be done instantly. The most intriguing skill of the cuttlefish, though, is the spectacular color display it sets off when it is excited.
